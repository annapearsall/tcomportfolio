Hello there!

I'm Anna, and I am about to graduate from Eastern Washington University with my Bachelor's of Arts in Technical Communication and a minor in Creative Writing. I have experience with a variety of technical writing documents, including instructions, grant writing, technical editing, and web writing. I also have experience in web design and development.

In my work, I have a great balance between my creative, thinking-big side, and my technical, attentive-to-detail side. I love working on projects that challenge me and push me to my limits. Web design in particular always excites me, motivates me, and can be daunting at times in the best way possible. I prefer short-term, contract-based work that grants me freedom to spontaneously go on adventures, like working summers in Southeast Alaska!
